    Quota & Flexibility

    Flexible Minutes & Mobile Internet Usage

    With WE Gold, use your minutes and mobile internet allowance with more flexibility

    • Local minutes to WE or other networks will be charged 1:1
    • Mobile internet will be charged 1:1
    • If you run out of mobile internet, you can get mobile gigabytes using your minutes bundle with a rate (1 mobile megabyte = 1 minute)
    • If you run out of mobile internet and minutes, you can get mobile gigabytes using your fixed internet bundle with a rate (1 mobile megabyte = 20 fixed internet megabytes)
    • No separate allowance for SMS, it will be deducted from local minutes allowance (1 SMS = 1 minute)
    • Unused mobile internet gigabytes will be carried over to the following month
    • If you consume all the minutes and mobile internet, you can follow any of the below options:
      • You will be charged for the mobile internet by any of the below options:
        • Subscribe in any of Nitro add-ons
        • Or paying 5 EGP for every 200 MBs
      • You will be charged 14 Pts to 1 minute or 1 SMS.

    Travel Experience

    Unique Travel Experience With WE Gold:

    Enjoy traveling abroad with WE Gold and get international minutes and data roaming all year round

    1. International Minutes Bundle:

    • Starting WE Gold 600, you will be granted monthly international minutes.
    • International minutes are not carried over to the next month.
    • Upon consuming your granted minutes, you will be charged with current international minute rates.
    • International minutes are available exclusively to WE Gold customers.
    • Current roaming receiving minutes rates will apply.
    • The minutes are not eligible for usage in the "hard to reach" countries. For more details visit https://te.eg/sl/InterRoaming

    2. Data Roaming Bundle:

    • WE Gold customers are eligible for two data roaming bundles twice a year, and every bundle is valid for one week from the time of activating the bundle.
    • The data roaming quota differs according to each price point.
    • The maximum data roaming bundles are 2 bundles per year.
    • You can add extra data roaming bundles with extra fees that will be added to your monthly bill.
    • You can activate the data roaming bundle through My WE app, *800# or by calling 500.
    • The MBs are not eligible for usage in the "hard to reach" countries. For more details visit https://te.eg/sl/InterRoaming

    3. WE Flight:

    • Starting WE Gold 1500, enjoy outgoing calls to Egypt and the rest of the world by dialing *800*11# during your flight as per the below table:
    WE Gold Number of outgoing minutes per year
    WE Gold 1500 4 Minutes / twice a year
    WE Gold 2300 6 Minutes / 3 times a year
    • To know more about WE Flight service eligible airlines, please visit https://te.eg/sl/WEFlight.
    • You should activate the WE Flight add-on through My WE app or by dialing *800#.
    • After consuming the minutes, you will be charged with the out-of-bundle WE Flight minute rate. Please visit https://te.eg/sl/WEFlight for more details.

    Follow the below steps during the flight:

    1. Switch your phone on, make sure airplane mode is off.
    2. Your phone will automatically connect to the AeroMobile network. The network name may be different according to the mobile.
    3. If your phone does not connect to the AeroMobile network automatically, you will need to go to 'Carrier' in your phone settings and manually select AeroMobile.
    4. Once connected, you'll receive a welcome text from AeroMobile. You'll likely receive a text message from your mobile network operator.
    5. You can now use your WE Flight minutes.

    How To Join WE Gold

    How To Join WE Gold With Your Number

    • To migrate from other networks to WE Gold, you must visit the nearest WE branch and the migration process will be completed within only 24 hours.

    Enjoy Sharing Your Bundle With Your Family

    • You can share your minutes and mobile gigabytes with up to 6 members according to each price point
    • You can add extra members to family group with 10 EGP per member and the fees will to be added to your monthly bill
    • Family members can be subscribers of any of the following plans (12Pts, Kix, Super Kix, New Kix, Kix Plus, Tazbeet, Indigo 7.5, 52, 120, 260, 430 or 860)
    • You can add family members and share your bundle through My WE app,*800# or call the call center on 500 for more details.
    • Once the member is added to the family group, you should add consumption limit to the minutes and mobile gigabytes.
    • Consumption limits are renewed automatically on monthly basis. In case the family group owner adjusted the limit, the new limit will be renewed automatically.
    • You can also pay the mobile payments of your family members either partially or fully.
    • Family group owner can modify or delete the members. Members also can remove themselves from the group.
    • Family group members can’t either join any other family groups nor he/she could be an owner of a another family group
    • Family group members will be granted 10,000 intra-minutes to make calls between each other and between members and owner only.
    • Family group owner can’t share add-ons with group members as add-ons are consumed by group owner only except when subscribing in Nitro Family add-ons

    WE Bonus Monthly Points

    • You will accumulate monthly points to be redeemable at WE stores and WE Bonus partners network
    • Each WE Gold bundle will be granted monthly bonus points when paying the monthly invoice, excluding taxes and installments.
    • You can redeem every 100 points for 5 EGP at WE store or through WE Bonus partners network
    • Points are not transferred or assigned to any other number. In case the line is deactivated, the use of the monthly points will be stopped.
    • Points are valid for 2 years from the date of receiving them.
    • Know more about WE Bonus partners network and your points expiry date through My WE app.
    • All We Bonus services and application browsing are available only within Egypt.

    Device Annual Voucher

    • Starting WE Gold 600, you will receive an annual device voucher
    • The voucher's validity is just one year
    • The voucher will be redeemed with only on item from the available devices, accessories, and routers available in WE stores
    • The voucher can’t be used to buy a line, pay a bill or renew a bundle
    • In case of bundle upgrading or downgrading during the year you will receive the voucher according to the bundle with the longer period
    • You will be eligible to receive your first device voucher after paying 12 complete bills excluding prorated bills, after 12 months from joining WE Gold with condition that there are no due bills.
    • The voucher can be deducted partially or totally from the device value.
    • You can redeem your annual voucher at all B.Tech branches
    • Terms and conditions apply.
